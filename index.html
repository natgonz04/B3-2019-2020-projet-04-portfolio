About me. Let's work together !

Hi! I'm Natalia Gonzalez, a nomadic graphic designer & illustrator from Spain.

Currently: studying design and visual communication at ECV Bordeaux while traveling whenever I can. Previously interships : MAARTIN studio in Paris.

I'm a nearly graduated from École de Communication Visuelle of Bordeaux(France) in 2020 with a degree in Visual Communication. Animal gifs, chocolate and nearly any 90's tracks are my everyday fuel. What I do: Art direction, Visual identity, Branding, Packaging design, Editorial design, Illustration. What I don't: well-cooked meals, yoga without breaking my back (well, at all), pretending I love coffe.
